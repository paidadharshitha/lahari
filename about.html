About Lahari Legal Associates

A legacy of legal excellence serving Hyderabad and Telangana

Defending Justice Since 2011

Lahari Legal Associates stands as a pillar of legal excellence in the heart of Hyderabad. Founded with an unwavering commitment to justice, our firm has grown into one of Telangana's most trusted legal practices — handling over 5,000 cases with an exceptional success rate across civil, criminal, corporate, and family law.

We believe that every client deserves not just legal representation, but a relentless champion who fights for their rights as fiercely as if they were our own. Our team of experienced advocates combines deep legal expertise with genuine compassion, delivering results that protect what matters most to you.

The Firm

Lahari Legal Associates is a premier law firm based in Hyderabad, Telangana, recognized for delivering exceptional legal services across a comprehensive range of practice areas. With a team of experienced advocates and a commitment to results-driven representation, we have established ourselves as one of the most trusted legal practices in the region.

Our Story

Lahari Legal Associates was founded with a singular vision: to make world-class legal representation accessible to the people and businesses of Hyderabad and Telangana. Over the last 15 years, we have grown from a small practice into one of the region's most respected law firms, handling over 5,000 cases with a remarkable success rate.

Our approach combines deep legal expertise with genuine compassion for clients. That ethos remains at the heart of everything we do. Every case we take on receives the full weight of our knowledge, resources, and dedication.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional legal outcomes through a combination of deep domain expertise, innovative strategies, and unwavering ethical standards. We believe that every client deserves a champion who will fight for their rights as fiercely as if they were our own.

We are committed to continuous learning, adapting to evolving legal landscapes, and leveraging modern technology to serve our clients more effectively. Our mission drives every decision we make and every case we handle.

Our Experience

Key cases and achievements that demonstrate our legal expertise across diverse practice areas (2025-2026).

2025Civil Law

Civil Property Dispute Resolution

Successfully resolved a complex multi-party property dispute involving contested land titles across three generations. Our team achieved a favorable settlement through meticulous documentation, title verification, and strategic mediation, saving the client years of protracted litigation.

2025Criminal Law

Criminal Defense - Major Case Handling

Provided robust defense in a high-profile criminal case involving serious charges. Our strategic approach to evidence examination, witness preparation, and courtroom advocacy resulted in a favorable judgment for the client, protecting their liberty and reputation.

2025Corporate Law

Corporate Contract Litigation

Represented a mid-size IT company in a complex breach-of-contract dispute worth over 15 crores. Through aggressive negotiation and meticulous legal drafting, we secured a favorable settlement that protected our client's business interests and set a strong precedent.

2025Family Law

Family Law Settlements

Handled sensitive family law matters including divorce proceedings, child custody arrangements, and alimony negotiations with complete confidentiality and compassion. Achieved amicable settlements that prioritized the well-being of all parties involved.

2026Cybercrime

Cybercrime & Online Fraud Cases

Successfully prosecuted multiple cybercrime cases involving online financial fraud, identity theft, and digital harassment. Coordinated with cybercrime cells and technology forensics experts to build strong cases that led to conviction and asset recovery for victims.

2026Property Law

Property Recovery & Legal Disputes

Recovered high-value commercial property worth 25 crores through a combination of civil suits, documentation verification, and strategic legal action in the Hon'ble High Court. Demonstrated our strength in handling complex property recovery matters with precision.

2026Arbitration

Arbitration & Legal Advisory Success

Served as legal advisors in multiple arbitration proceedings, achieving favorable outcomes for corporate clients through expert mediation, strategic legal counsel, and comprehensive documentation. Our advisory services have become a trusted resource for businesses across Telangana.
