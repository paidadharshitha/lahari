Wills & Estate Planning

Comprehensive estate planning through wills, trusts, probate, and succession law to protect your legacy and provide for your loved ones.

Home > Services > Wills & Estate Planning

Overview

Estate planning is one of the most thoughtful and consequential decisions a person can make, yet it is also one of the most frequently overlooked. In India, where family structures are complex and property holdings often span multiple generations, the absence of a properly drafted will or trust can lead to protracted inheritance disputes that drain family wealth and fracture relationships for decades. Lahari Legal Associates offers comprehensive wills and trusts services, from drafting simple testamentary documents to establishing sophisticated private and public trusts for wealth preservation and succession planning.

Our practice encompasses the full lifecycle of estate planning: initial consultation to understand your family structure, assets, and wishes; drafting and execution of wills that comply with the Indian Succession Act and applicable personal laws; creation of revocable and irrevocable trusts for minors, dependents, or charitable purposes; probate and succession certificate applications; and litigation when wills are contested by dissatisfied heirs. We also advise NRI clients on cross-border estate planning concerns, including the interplay between Indian succession laws and foreign inheritance regulations.

Our Process

1

Asset & Family Mapping

We conduct a thorough inventory of all your assets — immovable property, bank accounts, investments, insurance policies, business interests, and digital assets — and map them against your intended beneficiaries to create a comprehensive estate plan.

2

Will or Trust Drafting

Based on your objectives, we draft a legally compliant will with precise bequest clauses, guardian nominations for minors, and executor appointments — or alternatively, structure a trust deed with detailed terms governing asset management and distribution.

3

Execution & Registration

We ensure proper execution with the required two witnesses, arrange voluntary registration with the Sub-Registrar for added legal weight, securely store copies, and provide guidance on safe custody of the original document.

4

Probate, Succession & Dispute Resolution

When the testator passes, we handle probate applications before the competent court, obtain succession certificates where needed, and vigorously defend the will against any probate challenges or family claims through litigation if required.
