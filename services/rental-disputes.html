PRACTICE AREA

Rental Disputes

Resolving landlord-tenant conflicts, eviction proceedings, lease enforcement, and rent control matters across Telangana.

Home > Services > Rental Disputes

Overview

Hyderabad's booming real estate market has made rental disputes one of the most frequently litigated areas of civil law in Telangana. Whether you are a landlord struggling with a tenant who has stopped paying rent, refuses to vacate after the lease term, or has sublet your property without consent — or a tenant facing illegal eviction, arbitrary rent hikes, or withholding of your security deposit — Lahari Legal Associates provides balanced, effective legal representation for both parties under the Telangana Buildings (Lease, Rent and Eviction) Control Act and the Transfer of Property Act.

Our rental dispute practice extends beyond courtroom litigation. We help landlords draft watertight lease agreements that anticipate common points of contention, establish proper security deposit protocols, and include enforceable termination clauses. For tenants, we ensure that your rights under rent control legislation are fully protected and that landlords cannot circumvent legal procedures through coercion, utility cutoffs, or illegal lockout tactics. Our goal is to resolve rental conflicts efficiently while preserving the commercial relationship between parties whenever possible.

Our Process

1

Lease Agreement & Rental History Review

We examine the existing lease agreement, rent payment records, correspondence between the parties, and any prior oral or written agreements to establish the precise terms, identify breaches, and determine the applicable legal framework.

2

Notice & Negotiation

We issue a legally compliant eviction notice or demand notice as required, and simultaneously attempt structured negotiation to reach a mutually acceptable resolution — such as a voluntary vacation timeline, arrears payment schedule, or lease modification.

3

Rent Controller or Civil Court Filing

If negotiations fail, we file an eviction petition before the Rent Controller or a civil suit for eviction and arrears recovery before the appropriate court, presenting documentary evidence and witness testimony to support your claim.

4

Execution & Possession Handover

Upon obtaining a favourable order, we coordinate with court bailiffs for physical possession delivery, ensure arrears and damages are recovered through execution proceedings, and handle any stay applications or appeals filed by the opposing party.
