Cybercrime & Online Fraud

Defending victims of online fraud, identity theft, data breaches, and digital crimes under the Information Technology Act.

Home > Services > Cybercrime & Online Fraud

Overview

As India's digital economy expands at an unprecedented pace, cybercrime has emerged as one of the fastest-growing categories of legal disputes. From UPI fraud and phishing scams to sophisticated identity theft rings and corporate data breaches, the threat landscape evolves faster than most individuals and businesses can adapt. Lahari Legal Associates provides specialised cybercrime defence and prosecution services, leveraging our deep understanding of the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Indian Penal Code's digital offence provisions, and emerging data protection regulations.

Our cybercrime practice serves both individual victims — those who have lost money to online scams, suffered reputational harm through social media defamation, or had their personal data compromised — as well as corporate clients dealing with ransomware attacks, intellectual property theft, insider data breaches, and regulatory compliance investigations. We work alongside certified digital forensics experts to preserve electronic evidence, trace transactions across cryptocurrency networks, and build legally sound cases that withstand the rigorous standards of Indian criminal courts.

Our Process

1

Incident Assessment & Evidence Preservation

We immediately assess the scope of the cyber incident, guide you through critical first steps like preserving server logs and screenshots, and engage digital forensics experts to create legally admissible copies of all electronic evidence before it can be altered or destroyed.

2

FIR Filing & Regulatory Complaints

We draft and file a detailed First Information Report with the local cybercrime police station, coordinate with the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) where applicable, and lodge complaints with regulatory bodies such as the RBI for financial fraud or MeitY for data violations.

3

Investigation Support & Case Building

Our team collaborates with law enforcement agencies and private investigators to trace IP addresses, analyse financial trails, identify perpetrators, and compile a comprehensive chargesheet-ready dossier that maximises the likelihood of successful prosecution.

4

Court Representation & Asset Recovery

We represent you throughout the criminal trial, file interim applications for asset freezing or account garnishment, pursue civil remedies for damages alongside the criminal proceedings, and handle appeals to ensure complete legal redress.
