About the Firm

Rooted in Justice, Driven by Excellence

Lahari Legal Associates was established with a singular vision: to provide accessible, strategic, and uncompromising legal representation to clients across Hyderabad and Telangana. Our practice is built on the pillars of ethical advocacy, client-centric service, and meticulous attention to every case.

Under the leadership of Adv. Lahari Gadamsetty, our firm has grown from a boutique practice into a comprehensive legal services provider — handling complex civil, criminal, corporate, and consumer matters with equal proficiency.

Our Mission

To safeguard the rights of our clients through diligent legal representation, transparent communication, and unwavering commitment to justice and ethical practice.

Our Vision

To be recognized as one of Hyderabad's most trusted law firms — known for integrity, excellence, and impactful legal outcomes that make a difference.

Meet Our Lead Counsel

Experienced legal professionals committed to your case.

Adv. Lahari Gadamsetty

Founding Advocate & Legal Consultant

Adv. Lahari Gadamsetty is a practicing advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Telangana, with extensive experience in civil litigation, corporate advisory, consumer protection, and family law. Known for her meticulous preparation and strategic approach, she has successfully represented clients before the Hon'ble High Court of Telangana, District Courts, Consumer Forums, and various tribunals.

Her practice philosophy centers on accessible justice — ensuring that every client receives clear legal counsel, transparent communication, and the strongest possible representation.

"The law exists to protect the vulnerable, uphold truth, and ensure that no voice goes unheard. My practice is committed to these principles — every case, every client, every time."

— Adv. Lahari Gadamsetty
